Hi, I'm Matty!

♦ Scientist and Artist based in Edinburgh, UK ♦

I'm a doctoral student at The University of Edinburgh, working in the areas of artificial intelligence, neuroscience, psychiatry and robotics.

What really interests me is PERCEPTION, and how it SHAPES the experiences of OUR LIVES. I believe both SCIENCE and ART have incredible and complementary potential to help us understand and shape perception.

In addition to my scientific research, PHOTOGRAPHY, POETRY and MUSIC are fun and reflective practices I use to better understand and expand my own perception of life.

Whilst I enjoy working on each art form seperately, I am currently working on short films that integrate all three. You can find my latest films below.

You can also find a selection of photographs in my GALLERY, and a selection of poems in my POETRY COLLECTION.
