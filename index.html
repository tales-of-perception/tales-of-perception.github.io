About

Artist based in Edinburgh, UK

Life can be difficult.

I practice art as a means to process, understand, and explore perceptual, emotional and mental landscapes.

Photography, music and poetry are my primary artistic practices.

Beyond art, I have a diverse background, spanning areas such as robotics, AI, neuroscience, and engineering, and am currently working towards a PhD in Artificial Intelligence and Psychiatry at the University of Edinburgh.
