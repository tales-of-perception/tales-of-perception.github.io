Chlo

X

Timeless Chlo
Floats through here;
I see her cling
To old stone
From told Rome,
Turned new, New Jórvík,
new beauty.

Her spirit art –
Bright poetic stars –
Collide and halve;
One piece melts
Inside
The heart
Of Minster's alter.

The other is hers to keep.
