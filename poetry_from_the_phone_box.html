Poetry from the Phone Box

What do we want from our information technologies? Phone boxes at one time offered millions of people the opportunity to connect in real time across space. But in an age of hyperconnectivity through the internet, phone boxes have become a defunct technology. This series aims to highlight our relationship to information technology, by telling stories using a technology that has faded just as quickly as it had emerged.
