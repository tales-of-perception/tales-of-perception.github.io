amtix

amtix is a musical project inspired by the many nights I've spent wandering streets, often with my camera in hand, and usually lost in thought. The sound is ambient and atmospheric-led, intended to evoke space, reflection, and maybe a little meloncholy.

The music in this project is distinct from the music released under my main artist name (Matty Mattix).

Listen on Spotify, Apple Music, or whichever streaming platform you prefer.
